About Me

My Story

Once lost, now found in design, an Empathetic Designer creating experiences that truly resonate.

I was born on a small island called Saipan (12.5 miles long and 5.5 miles wide) I know...tiny. Eventually, I moved to Las Vegas, where I spent most of my life. My path to design wasn't linear; in fact, it was anything but traditional.

I once aspired to be a Crime Scene Investigator. Instead, I spent over 5 years working in customer service across the food and retail industries, with my last role as a Visual Stylist. That position became a turning point, I discovered a passion for design and was filled with ideas on how to create better experiences for people. However, I lacked the technical skills to bring those ideas to life.

To bridge that gap, I completed a Full Stack Development bootcamp, but it wasn't until I discovered UX Design that everything clicked. Initially, I tried the self taught route, but as I navigated my own learning style, I realized I needed structure to stay on track. Enrolling in a UX Program provided me with the foundation and focus I needed to grow.

Looking back, I once believed I was lost, jumping from one career aspiration to another, unable to find my true calling. But in reality, there was always a common thread..service. Whether it was CSI, where I sought justice for others, customer service, where I aimed to make people's experiences better, or now UX Design where I create intuitive solutions to improve lives, I was always drawn to helping people.

Every step of my journey has shaped me into a more empathetic designer. My diverse experiences have given me a deeper understanding of people from all walks of life, allowing me to craft meaningful, human-centered solutions.

What once felt like uncertainty was, in truth, the path that prepared me for exactly where I'm meant to be today.
