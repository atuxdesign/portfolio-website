About Me

My Story

I was born on the small island of Saipan and later moved to Las Vegas, where I spent most of my life. My path to design wasn’t linear; in fact it was anything but traditional. I once aspired to be a Crime Scene Investigator but ended up spending over five years in customer service and retail, with my last role as a Visual Stylist. That role sparked my passion for design, but I lacked the technical skills to bring my ideas to life.

To bridge that gap, I completed a Full Stack Development certification, but it wasn't till later on down the road where I discovered UX Design and that was when things truly clicked. Initially self-taught, I realized I needed structure, so I enrolled in a UX Design program to build a strong foundation.

Looking back, my career shifts always had a common thread...service. Whether seeking justice in CSI, enhancing experiences in customer service, or now designing intuitive solutions, I’ve always been driven by helping people.

What once felt like uncertainty was actually the path that led me exactly where I’m meant to be today.
